Offered Services

Criminal Cases
Criminal cases are legal proceedings where the government prosecutes individuals for violating penal laws, with penalties including imprisonment, fines, or probation.
Civil Cases
Civil cases are legal disputes between individuals, businesses, or government entities that do not involve criminal acts, focusing instead on resolving conflicts, such as breach of contract, personal injury (torts), property disputes, or family matters.
Corporate Cases
Corporate cases involve legal disputes, compliance matters, and scandals impacting companies, ranging from intracorporate, fraud, and breach of fiduciary duties to data privacy and regulatory violations.
